FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers.

How much is the 12-week web and mobile development course?

85,000kes. Compared to our competitors, we are offering an immersive program where you will have access to mentors and support 24/7.

Will I get a job out of your programs?

That depends on you! While we provide world-class teachers and experts to support you, the learning is on you. We’re putting in our 200% and we expect you to put in yours as well. At the end of the program, we will have a career fair where you showcase your portfolio to potential employers.

What kind of students are you looking for?

We’re looking for pro-active, open-minded students who are willing to be stretched and challenged. Learning how to code isn’t easy, but the right attitude can help you go a long way. Students need to have basic math skills and logic. More importantly, we are looking for people who want to see others succeed and therefore see the best in others. We want people who are excited to take what they learn from their course and use it to launch a career that they love.

Where are you located?

We are located in Nairobi, Kenya

Do you offer scholarships for your courses?

We only offer 2 partial scholarships (25% of tuition) for the first class. get in touch with us, if you are interested.

What will I learn at Moringa School that I can’t learn at school?

At Moringa School, we believe in your potential. While like universities, we teach a little of theory to help you understand the different languages and their macro-importance, we’re more interested in teaching you practical skills that will make you a valuable hire and also able to start your own business.
