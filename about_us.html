About Moringa School This is who we are and what we stand for.

moringa school is where:

A whole generation of high-potential, pro-active people living in emerging markets are being enabled to pursue their dreams by equipping them with various skills to do so. We are keen on levelling the playing ground for people in emerging markets by empowering the students with a world-class curriculum conducted with top quality teachers and mentors to support them through the process. We offer a full-time, comprehensive web and mobile development course, our part-time courses on topics such as product management or digital marketing and day-long workshops.
